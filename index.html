About Reflo

Leading the way in sustainable technology since 2020

Reflo was founded with a simple mission: to reduce electronic waste while providing high-quality, affordable technology products. We take pre-owned devices, restore them to like-new condition through our rigorous certification process, and offer them at a fraction of the cost of new devices.

Our commitment to sustainability goes beyond just refurbishing devices. We use eco-friendly packaging, power our facilities with renewable energy, and partner with e-waste recycling programs to ensure proper disposal of components that cannot be reused.

Every refurbished device we sell represents a significant reduction in environmental impact compared to manufacturing a new device. Electronic devices require substantial resources to produce, including rare earth minerals, water, and energy.

By extending the lifecycle of these devices, we're not only reducing e-waste but also decreasing the demand for new manufacturing, which helps conserve natural resources and reduce carbon emissions.

Our commitment to sustainability extends beyond just refurbishing devices:

  • We use 100% recyclable, plastic-free packaging materials
  • Our facilities are powered by renewable energy
  • We partner with certified e-waste recyclers for components that cannot be reused
  • We plant a tree for every device sold through our partnership with reforestation initiatives
  • We offer trade-in programs to ensure proper recycling of old devices

Reflo Certified Quality

Our rigorous testing process ensures premium quality

Every device that carries the Reflo Certified label has undergone a comprehensive inspection and testing process to ensure it meets our high standards for quality, performance, and cosmetic condition.

71-Point Check for Laptops

  • Battery health verification (min. 85% capacity)
  • Display quality and pixel inspection
  • Keyboard and trackpad functionality
  • Port and connection testing
  • Wi-Fi, Bluetooth and network performance
  • Storage device speed and health check
  • Memory integrity verification
  • Processor and GPU benchmark tests

50-Point Check for Mobiles

  • Battery health verification (min. 90% capacity)
  • Screen quality and touch response
  • Camera functionality and quality
  • Speaker and microphone testing
  • Biometric sensors verification
  • All buttons functionality check
  • Cellular and wireless connectivity tests
  • Water resistance integrity (where applicable)

After passing all tests, devices receive professional cleaning, sanitization, and final cosmetic inspection before being certified ready for their new home.
